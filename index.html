Room Tour

Explore every corner of your mountain retreat

Living Room

Stone fireplace, plush blue sectional, and French doors opening to panoramic mountain views. The perfect gathering space with rustic charm and modern comfort.

Kitchen & Dining

Fully equipped kitchen with maple cabinets, stainless steel appliances, and island seating. Dining room seats 8+ with large picture windows overlooking the lake.

Main Level King Suite

Spacious king bedroom with balcony access and en-suite bathroom featuring a walk-in shower. Perfect for the primary guests seeking privacy and convenience.

Upper Level King Suite

Luxurious king bedroom with elegant pine furnishings and an en-suite bathroom boasting a jetted soaking tub. A serene retreat within your retreat.

Loft Suite

A showstopper with vaulted ceilings, exposed wood beams, and stunning triangular picture windows framing panoramic lake views. An unforgettable master bedroom experience.

Seating Room & Workspace

Comfortable daybed with lake-view windows and dedicated desk space. Ideal for remote work or a quiet reading nook with inspiration on all sides.

Game Room & Family Room

Two entertainment-packed spaces: ping pong, pool table, arcade machines, and karaoke in the game room; stone fireplace, leather sofas, and hot tub access in the family room.

Entry & Mudroom

Welcoming entryway with grand staircase and convenient mudroom with coat storage and bench seating. Wheelchair-accessible ramp leads to the front door for easy access.

Exterior & Outdoors

A wraparound deck with panoramic lake and mountain views, covered hot tub, outdoor dining, and a private boat slip just minutes away at Deep Creek Lake.
